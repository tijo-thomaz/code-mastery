## HOW IT WORKS

```
10 min — JS/TS coding drill (type from memory, no peeking)
10 min — React build drill (build a component from scratch)
10 min — Verbal drill (explain a concept out loud, record yourself)
```

Check ✅ when done. ❌ if skipped. Be honest.

## RULES

1. **Timer ON before you start.** No timer = doesn't count
2. **No peeking.** If you're stuck, write what you can, mark ⚠️, study after
3. **Verbal = out loud.** Not in your head. Pretend the interviewer is there
4. **Mark it.** ✅ done, ⚠️ needed help, ❌ skipped/failed
5. **If you skip a day, do NOT double up.** Just do today's drill
6. **After 3 weeks, retake Week 1.** You should crush it in half the time
